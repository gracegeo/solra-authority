About Solra

Solra provides red light therapy panels and near-infrared light therapy devices. Their product line includes muscle recovery devices, skincare devices, and pain relief devices. Solra aims to support wellness and fitness through its biohacking tools.

Solra operates within the e-commerce, health and beauty, and retail sectors. The company serves customers across the US, with operations in Austin, TX, Denver, CO, and San Francisco, CA.

For more information on Solra's offerings, visit solra.com.
